02 How long will the recruitment process take?
+
The recruitment process typically takes 2-4 weeks from application to offer. This includes initial screening, interviews, and final decision-making. For more senior positions, the process may take longer due to additional interview rounds and assessments.
04 Do you recruit for Graduates, Apprentices and Students?
+
Yes, we actively recruit graduates, apprentices, and students. We offer specialized programs designed to help those early in their careers develop professional skills and gain valuable experience. Our AI tools are particularly helpful for those with limited interview experience.
03 What does the recruitment and selection process involve?
+
Our recruitment process typically involves CV screening, AI-powered skills assessment, initial phone interview, technical or skills assessment, panel interview, and final decision. Each step is designed to evaluate different aspects of your qualifications and fit for the role.
05 Can I receive notifications for any future jobs that may interest me?
+
Yes, you can set up job alerts to receive notifications about positions matching your skills and interests. Our AI system will analyze your profile and send relevant opportunities directly to your email. You can customize the frequency and types of jobs you'd like to be notified about.
